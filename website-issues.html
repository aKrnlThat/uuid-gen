Here you can find common website issues and how to resolve them, and also the issues that have been found and will be fixed later.

Common issues (not our fault)

Some other extensions such as Dark Reader may be impacting the dark mode. Try turning them off. Also, the accordions (like this one and the one in the changelog) use Bootstrap, which background classes will slightly break accordions.

Known issues (fixing later)

Technically, settings still apply, for now you have to make a mental note while the issue is diagnosed.
